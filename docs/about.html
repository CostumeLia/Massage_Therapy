About Jake

Your Partner in Wellness

Hello, I'm Jake. My journey into massage therapy began with a simple belief: the power of touch can be a profound source of healing. After experiencing its benefits firsthand, I dedicated myself to mastering the art and science of massage to help others find relief and relaxation.

I graduated from the National Institute of Massage Therapy and have since become certified in various techniques, including Swedish, Deep Tissue, and Sports Massage. My approach is client-centered, meaning every session is tailored to your specific needs and wellness goals. I believe in creating a safe, compassionate, and tranquil space where you can truly unwind and let go of stress.

My goal is to not only address your immediate discomfort but also to empower you with a greater sense of body awareness that you can carry into your daily life. I look forward to being a part of your wellness journey.
